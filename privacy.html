Passera Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Passera ("the app") handles your information. Passera is designed for local, on-device toll guidance and does not require an account.

1. Information We Process

  • Precise Location (if you grant permission): Used to detect nearby toll portals and show relevant toll pricing in your selected city.
  • App Settings: Your selected city, language, and notification preference are stored on your device.
  • Notification Data (optional): If enabled, the app schedules local reminders on your device for selected toll time windows.
  • Passage Log Data (if used by the app): Toll passage records such as timestamp, city/zone, portal identifier, amount, and waived flag are stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • Calculate and display current and upcoming toll prices.
  • Monitor nearby toll zones using iOS location services.
  • Provide optional local notifications you request.
  • Store app preferences and local records to support app features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with data brokers. The app is designed to process data locally on device.

4. Tracking and Advertising

Passera does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites for targeted advertising.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Data described above is stored locally on your device. It is retained until you remove it (for example by clearing app data or uninstalling the app).

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable Location access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can disable Notifications at any time in iOS Settings or app settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and uninstall it.

7. Children's Privacy

Passera is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. International Transfers

Because Passera is designed for on-device processing, we do not transfer personal data to external servers for core app functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the App Support contact listed on the Passera App Store product page.
