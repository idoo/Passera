## Common Questions

- **Why is location permission needed?**  
  Passera uses location to detect nearby toll portals and show relevant toll pricing.

- **Why am I not getting reminders?**  
  Check that notifications are enabled both in Passera settings and in iOS Settings.

- **Why can Stockholm prices differ?**  
  Stockholm has multiple zones (e.g., Inner City and Essingeleden) with different rates.

- **Can I use Passera without an account?**  
  Yes. Passera does not require account creation for core features.

## Troubleshooting

- Open iOS Settings and confirm Location access for Passera is granted.
- Open iOS Settings and confirm Notifications are allowed for Passera.
- Restart the app after changing location or notification permissions.
- Update to the latest app version from the App Store.
